Our Story

Honest Repairs
Since 1989

Service Scene has been serving Melbourne's Eastern Suburbs for over 35 years as an authorised service centre for audio and visual equipment through Tarnel Pty Ltd.

We're proud members of the Electronic Service Centres Association (ESCA) and authorised to repair and service products from leading brands including Panasonic, Toshiba and LG.

Our promise is simple: we'll always give you an honest assessment. We evaluate each job individually and only recommend repairs that make financial sense for you. No unnecessary work, no surprises.
